How too Surf

Learning how to surf is fun. Follow these steps below to see how too:

  1. Center yourself on the Surfboard.

    2. Making sure that you are balanced on the board helps you later for when you try and stand.

  2. Align yourself with the center of the wave where it starts to break
  3. Face the opposite direction from the wave
  4. Paddle until you feel the wave pushing you forward

    5. Knowing when you have "caught" the wave is the hardest part

  5. Stand up

It is that easy to surf!
